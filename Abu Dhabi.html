Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates

One of the most popular tourist destinations in the world is Dubai. An alternative to the busyness and extravagance of Dubai is the city of Abu Dhabi. Viewed as the holier and more traditional of the two cities, Abu Dhabi is the ideal destination for anyone loking to avoid the maddening crowds of Dubai.The following are four things to do in Abu Dhabi:

  1. Sign Up for a Desert Safari
  2. Visit Ferrari World
  3. Make memories on Yas Island
  4. Admire the brilliance of the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque
