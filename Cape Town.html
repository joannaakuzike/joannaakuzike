Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town is the legislative capital of South Africa and lies at the northern end of the Cape Peninsula. Popular for its beautiful settings and tranquil beach, Cape Town is the perfect destination for families looking for a change in scenery. The city has a mix of cultures and has so much to offer from restaurants to entertainment centres. Below lies four things to do in the mother city of Cape Town:

  1. Visit the iconic Table Mountain
  2. Learn about the history of apartheid on Robben Island
  3. Have lunch at the Neighbourgoods Market
  4. Take a stroll on the Boulders Beach
