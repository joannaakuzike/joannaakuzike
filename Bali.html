Bali, Indonesia

Characterized by oceanic views and vibrant nature, Bali is one of the most famous islands in Indonesia. It is also home to some of the most sacred temples in the world. Visiting Bali is both a spiritual and relaxing experience that I would recommend to any traveler. Here are four things you can do in Bali:

  1. Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary
  2. Hike on Mount Agung
  3. Have a relaxing rest at the Double Six Beach
  4. View the art at the Nyaman Gallery
